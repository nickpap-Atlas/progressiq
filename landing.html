What it does

Everything serious
trackers actually need.

Built from a real 6-month cut. Every feature earned its place.

🧪
Smart targets
Calculates your TDEE from actual body composition. Sets a 3-phase progressive deficit with protein locked throughout to protect lean mass.
📊
Weekly review
Rate of loss, calorie and protein compliance, training sessions — per week. Auto-suggests a calorie adjustment if you're losing too fast or too slow.
🏋
Program-aware training
Your exact program is loaded. Every exercise shows last session's numbers side by side. Rest timer, ATH tracking, progressive overload — all automatic.
🫚
Blood test parsing
Upload your pathology PDF and every biomarker is extracted automatically. Red/amber/green flags, trend tracking, DEXA scan integration.
📸
Progress photos
Weekly check-in photos stored privately. Front, side, back. Organised by week and visible to your trainer.
🗼
Full roadmap
Not just a log — a forward-looking plan. Week-by-week targets from deficit through reverse diet to surplus, with projected weights.

FAQ

Common questions.

Is this an app I need to download?
No. It runs in your browser — on your phone, tablet, or laptop. No app store, no install. Just bookmark it.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored in a secure Supabase database with row-level security. Only you can see your data.
Does my trainer need an account?
No. You generate a private link in Settings and share it. They open the link and get a read-only view — no login required.
Which blood test labs does it support?
Currently optimised for Laverty Pathology and MeasureUp DXA scans. Manual entry works for any lab. More formats coming.
Is this medical advice?
No. Progress IQ is a personal tracking tool. Always consult a qualified professional before changing your diet or training.
